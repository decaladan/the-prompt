Privacy Policy for The Prompt

Last updated: March 6, 2026

Introduction

The Prompt ("we", "our", or "us") is a word trivia game developed by Dimatica. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal data. The Prompt does not require an account, does not collect your name, email, location, or any other personal information.

Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores your high score locally on your device using standard device storage. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

Third-Party Services

The Prompt does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

The Prompt is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from children or any other users.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: privacy@dimatica.com
