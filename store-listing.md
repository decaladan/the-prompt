## Full Description (4000 chars max)
The Prompt is a fast-paced word trivia game that tests your knowledge and reflexes.

How to play:
- A question prompt appears on screen
- Choose the correct answer from four options
- Answer before the timer runs out
- Build streaks for bonus points
- Each round gets faster - how long can you keep up?

Features:
- 50+ trivia questions across multiple categories
- Increasing difficulty as you progress
- Streak multiplier system for bonus points
- Track your personal best score
- Clean, modern design
- No ads, no in-app purchases
- Works completely offline
- No account required

The Prompt is perfect for quick gaming sessions. Challenge yourself to beat your high score, or pass the phone to a friend and see who comes out on top.

Simple to learn, hard to master. Can you answer all 15 prompts?
